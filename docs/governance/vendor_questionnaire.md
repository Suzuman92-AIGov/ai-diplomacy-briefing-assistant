# Vendor Questionnaire

Core questions for LLM, embedding, database, hosting or AI service providers.

1. Are customer prompts, retrieved documents or generated briefs used to train or improve models?
2. Can data retention be disabled or configured?
3. Where is customer data stored?
4. Are enterprise confidentiality commitments available?
5. Are audit logs available?
6. Is role-based access control supported?
7. Are subprocessors listed?
8. Is there a Data Processing Agreement?
9. Are SOC 2, ISO 27001 or similar reports available?
10. How are incidents reported to customers?
11. Can customer data be deleted on request?
12. Are model changes communicated to customers?
